Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is handled in WorkTimer (hereinafter, “the App”).

Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information. All process templates and measurement data created within the App are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to any external server.

Purpose of Use

Data stored on your device is used solely to provide the App’s features (saving and reviewing measurement records).

Disclosure to Third Parties

The App does not provide, sell, or share any personal information or usage data with any third party.

Analytics and Advertising

The App does not use any analytics tools or advertising SDKs.
